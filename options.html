YouTube Ambilight

The settings are accessible from the YouTube video player.

I see no settings button after installation!

Please click on the refresh button in the browser or press the F5 shortkey.

If after the refresh the Ambilight settings button is still not shown you can create an issue on the issues list and I'll respond within 24 hours on workdays. Alternatively if you have no GitHub account leave your feedback via the Feedback form

Why do I see no Ambilight settings button after installation?

The extension is not loaded on YouTube webpages that were already open before the installation. So please click on the refresh button in the browser or press the F5 shortkey to load the extension.
